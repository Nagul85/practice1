Overview

Purva Soukhyam is the largest plotted development in the suburb of Chennai-Guduvancheri, presenting a harmonious blend of wellness and luxury living.

This one-of-a-kind development comes with an innovative concept that places community-wide wellness at its core.

With easy access to roadways, transportation hubs and existing/upcoming social infrastructure, Guduvancheri is just a short 25-minute drive away from iconic landmarks such as the Vandalur Zoo, and Vellore Institute of Technology ensuring that you're well-connected to the pulse of Chennai’s dynamic city life.
